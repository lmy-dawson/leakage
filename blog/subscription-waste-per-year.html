The subscription creep problem

Even if you audit your subscriptions today and cancel everything unnecessary, the problem comes back. This is called subscription creep â€” the gradual accumulation of charges over time that happens to everyone.

It works like this:

  • You sign up for a free trial of something useful
  • You forget to cancel before it converts to paid
  • Six months later you're still paying for it without realising
  • Meanwhile a service you already pay for quietly raises its price by $3
  • And you sign up for something else during a sale

Within a year you're back to where you started â€” or worse. The only way to break the cycle is continuous monitoring, not periodic audits.
